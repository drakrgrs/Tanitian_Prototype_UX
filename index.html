Getting Around

Transportation Guide

From the Airport:

• Rental cars available at airport ($40/day)

• Taxis available in Taniti City

• Hotel shuttle services (inquire at booking)

Local Transport:

• Public buses: 5 AM - 11 PM daily ($2 per ride)

• Private bus tours available

• Bike rentals ($5/day, helmets required)

• Taniti City is very walkable

Practical Information

Currency: US Dollar (also accepts euros and yen)

Power: 120 volts (same as USA)

Drinking Age: 18+ (not strictly enforced)

Alcohol Hours: Served 9 AM - midnight

Language: English widely spoken by younger residents

Safety: Low violent crime; watch for petty theft

Best Time: Check local holidays as attractions may close
